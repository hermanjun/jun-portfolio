Hey, Welcome to Herman's Digital World

I am Jun(Herman) Luo. I teach chemistry, study computer science, and build simple tools to make learning and life easier.

I use this space to track my projects, share ideas, and reflect on what I’m learning along the way.

About Me

I started in science and education, and now I am transitioning into building software and digital tools.

I studied Pharmaceutical Engineering and later worked in A-Level education, starting as a teaching assistant and becoming a full teacher with over 2500 hours of experience. Through teaching, I learned how to explain complex ideas in a clear and structured way.

While preparing lessons and during my working holiday in New Zealand, I started to explore ideas more deeply. This curiosity led me to computer science. I realised that I am not only interested in understanding systems, but also in building them.
